Our Story

Founded in 2020, Sensibolt emerged from a simple vision: to make IT service management accessible and intuitive for organizations of all sizes. What started as a small team of passionate IT professionals has grown into a leading provider of ITSM solutions.

We recognized that existing IT service management tools were complex, expensive, and difficult to implement. We decided to change that by creating a modern, user-friendly platform that puts efficiency and simplicity first.

Today, Sensibolt serves hundreds of organizations worldwide, from startups to Fortune 500 companies, helping them streamline their IT operations and deliver better service to their users.
